Typemory

A technilogical solution to the complicated, difficult, and often mundane task of memorizing long passages.

Really, though, this is just a startup website for my CS 260 class at Brigham Young University. But hey, if you find it useful, I hope you continue to use it!

But interestingly enough, there is some background to this startup. I completed Basic Cadet Training at the United States Air Force Academy in the summer of 2018, during which time I had to memorize painstakingly long quotes in mere days, with little to no actual time to study. I would have dreamed of having a tool as useful as Typemory available to me during basic training. Although, I have to admit, even if it was available, I am sure my cadre would have been sure to lay out some dire consequences for anyone who was caught using it. But now that I have finished basic training, I have the luxury of using tools such as Typemory to help me memorize things, and I hope you will enjoy this luxury with me (assuming you aren't in basic training right now :).

I would like to mention that some of this code came from examples on bootstrap's website as well as some general outlines provided on previous homework assignments. However, I am the sole author of the website itself.
